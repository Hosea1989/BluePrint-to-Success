Introduction

Building mobile apps can be a lucrative career or side business. This guide covers the complete process from idea to app store.

Platform Choices

  • Native iOS (Swift): Best performance, Apple-only
  • Native Android (Kotlin): Best performance, Android-only
  • Cross-Platform (Flutter/React Native): One codebase, both platforms

Development Process

  1. Ideation: Validate your app idea with market research
  2. Design: Create wireframes and UI mockups
  3. Development: Build MVP with core features
  4. Testing: Beta test with real users
  5. Launch: Submit to App Store/Play Store
  6. Iterate: Improve based on feedback

Monetization Strategies

  • Freemium: Free app with in-app purchases
  • Subscription: Recurring revenue model
  • Paid Apps: One-time purchase
  • Ads: Display advertising

Marketing Your App

  • App Store Optimization (ASO)
  • Social media marketing
  • Content marketing and SEO
  • Paid user acquisition

Costs to Consider

  • Apple Developer Program: $99/year
  • Google Play Developer: $25 one-time
  • Backend hosting costs
  • Marketing budget
