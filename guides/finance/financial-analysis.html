Why Financial Analysis?

Financial analysis is crucial for making informed business and investment decisions. It offers high earning potential and diverse career paths.

Career Paths

  • Financial Analyst: Analyzes financial data. Salary: $60K-$120K+
  • Investment Banker: Provides financial services to corporations. Salary: $90K-$200K+
  • Portfolio Manager: Manages investment portfolios. Salary: $80K-$180K+
  • Risk Manager: Identifies and mitigates financial risks. Salary: $70K-$150K+

Key Skills

  • Excel and financial modeling
  • Financial statement analysis
  • Valuation methods (DCF, comparables)
  • Data visualization
  • Communication and presentation

Certifications

  • CFA (Chartered Financial Analyst): Gold standard for investment professionals
  • CPA (Certified Public Accountant): For accounting focus
  • FRM (Financial Risk Manager): For risk management

30-Day Learning Plan

  1. Week 1: Learn financial statement analysis
  2. Week 2: Study financial ratios and valuation methods
  3. Week 3: Build a financial model in Excel
  4. Week 4: Present analysis to peers for feedback
