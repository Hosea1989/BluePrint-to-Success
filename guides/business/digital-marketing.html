What is Digital Marketing?

Digital marketing is promoting products or services through digital channels. It's essential for any modern business to reach customers where they spend time‚Äîonline.

Key Channels

  • SEO: Optimize for search engines to get organic traffic
  • Content Marketing: Create valuable content to attract customers
  • Social Media: Build presence on relevant platforms
  • Email Marketing: Direct communication with your audience
  • Paid Ads: Google Ads, Facebook Ads, etc.

SEO Basics

  • Research keywords your audience searches for
  • Create quality content around those keywords
  • Optimize page titles and meta descriptions
  • Build backlinks from reputable sites
  • Ensure your site is mobile-friendly and fast

Content Marketing

  • Blog Posts: Educational content that helps your audience
  • Video: YouTube, TikTok, Instagram Reels
  • Podcasts: Audio content for commuters
  • Infographics: Visual content that's shareable

Email Marketing

  • Build an email list (offer value in exchange)
  • Segment your audience
  • Write compelling subject lines
  • Provide value, don't just sell
  • Track open rates and conversions

Paid Advertising

  • Google Ads: Reach people searching for solutions
  • Facebook/Instagram Ads: Target specific demographics
  • LinkedIn Ads: B2B marketing
  • Retargeting: Reach people who visited your site

Measuring Success

  • Traffic and page views
  • Conversion rate
  • Cost per acquisition
  • Return on ad spend (ROAS)
