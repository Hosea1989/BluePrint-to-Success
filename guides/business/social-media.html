Why Social Media Matters

  • Build brand awareness
  • Connect directly with customers
  • Drive traffic to your business
  • Establish authority in your niche
  • Generate leads and sales

Choosing Platforms

  • Instagram: Visual products, lifestyle brands, younger audience
  • TikTok: Short-form video, viral potential, Gen Z
  • LinkedIn: B2B, professional services, thought leadership
  • Twitter/X: News, tech, real-time engagement
  • YouTube: Long-form video, tutorials, evergreen content

Content Strategy

  • Value First: Educate, entertain, or inspire before selling
  • Consistency: Post regularly on a schedule
  • Authenticity: Be genuine, show personality
  • Engagement: Respond to comments, DMs, and mentions

Growth Tactics

  • Post when your audience is most active
  • Use relevant hashtags strategically
  • Collaborate with others in your niche
  • Create shareable content
  • Run giveaways and contests
  • Cross-promote across platforms

Content Ideas

  • Behind-the-scenes
  • How-to tutorials
  • User-generated content
  • Industry news and commentary
  • Q&A and polls
  • Success stories and case studies

Metrics to Track

  • Follower growth rate
  • Engagement rate (likes, comments, shares)
  • Reach and impressions
  • Click-through rate
  • Conversions from social
