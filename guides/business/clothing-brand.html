Starting a Clothing Brand

Getting Started

  • Find Your Niche: What makes your brand unique? Who's your target customer?
  • Define Your Brand: Mission, values, aesthetic, and story
  • Research Competition: What are others doing? How will you differentiate?

Business Setup

  1. Choose a memorable brand name
  2. Register your business (LLC recommended)
  3. Get necessary permits and licenses
  4. Set up business banking
  5. Create your brand identity (logo, colors, fonts)

Production Options

  • Print-on-Demand: Low risk, low investment (Printful, Printify)
  • Wholesale Blanks: Buy blanks and customize (lower margins)
  • Cut and Sew: Custom manufacturing (higher investment, unique products)
  • Dropshipping: Source from suppliers without holding inventory

Design Process

  • Create original designs or hire a designer
  • Source quality blanks (fit and fabric matter)
  • Order samples before bulk production
  • Test with your target audience

Selling Your Products

  • Website: Shopify, WooCommerce, or Squarespace
  • Marketplaces: Etsy, Amazon Handmade
  • Social Commerce: Instagram Shop, TikTok Shop
  • Pop-ups: Local markets and events

Marketing Your Brand

  • Build social media presence (Instagram, TikTok)
  • Collaborate with influencers
  • User-generated content from customers
  • Email marketing for launches and drops
  • Create a brand community

Startup Costs

  • Business registration: $50-$500
  • Website: $30-$300/month
  • Initial inventory: $500-$5,000+
  • Photography: $0-$500
  • Marketing budget: Variable
